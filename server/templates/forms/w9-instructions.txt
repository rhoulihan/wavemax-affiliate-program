W-9 Form Instructions for WaveMAX Affiliates
===========================================

IMPORTANT: The IRS W-9 form must be obtained directly from the IRS website to ensure you have the current version.

Download the current W-9 form from:
https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw9.pdf

Instructions:
1. Download the official W-9 form from the IRS website
2. Complete all required fields:
   - Name (as shown on your income tax return)
   - Business name (if different from above)
   - Tax classification
   - Address
   - Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN)
   - Signature and date

3. Save the completed form as a PDF
4. Log in to your WaveMAX Affiliate Dashboard
5. Navigate to "Tax Information" section
6. Upload your completed W-9 form

Note: Payments cannot be processed until your W-9 is verified by our administrative team.

For questions about completing the W-9 form, consult a tax professional or visit:
https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-w-9
