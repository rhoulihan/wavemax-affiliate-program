Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn as an affiliate?

Earnings vary based on volume and your delivery pricing. Affiliates earn 10% of all WDF orders plus any markup you set on delivery services.

What areas can I service?

You can service any area around our WaveMAX location in Austin. We recommend focusing on neighborhoods within a comfortable driving distance.

How are payments handled?

Customer payments are processed through our secure system. Affiliate commissions are calculated and paid out weekly.

Do I need special equipment?

No special equipment is needed. You'll need reliable transportation for pickups and deliveries, and we provide the laundry bags with barcodes.

How quickly can I start?

After your application is approved (typically within 48 hours), you can immediately begin registering customers and scheduling pickups.
