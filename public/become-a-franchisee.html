The path forward

From researcher to franchise owner.

Buying a WaveMAX laundromat franchise is one of the largest financial decisions you'll make in your career. Our franchise discovery process is designed to give you the time, the data, and the direct access you need to make that decision with confidence — and to give us the chance to evaluate whether you're the right fit for our brand.

Most laundromat franchise opportunities you'll evaluate are built around a transactional sales pitch: a smooth-talking franchise rep, a polished pitch deck, and pressure to sign before the next quarter closes. This is not that.

We move at the pace of the qualified candidate, not the pace of the sales calendar. We expect you to talk to existing owners. We expect you to visit our company-owned store. And we expect you to walk away if it isn't the right fit. The result is a process that typically takes 60–120 days from initial conversation to award — and a franchise system where the people who make it through are some of the strongest operators in the industry.

The path

Six steps to laundromat ownership.

A clear path designed to help you make an informed decision — and to help us partner with the right operators.

01

Initial Conversation

A 30-minute introductory call to learn about your goals, market interest, financial position, and timeline. We answer your initial questions, share top-line numbers, and decide together whether it makes sense to keep going.

02

Discovery & FDD Review

Receive our Franchise Disclosure Document. Review the model, financials, support structure, royalty fees, territory rights, and Item 19 numbers in detail. Take as long as you need.

03

Validation Calls

Speak directly with current WaveMAX franchise owners. Ask anything. Learn what real ownership looks like — the wins, the surprises, the lessons. We do not script these calls.

04

Discovery Day

Visit our company-owned store, meet the leadership team, walk the operations end-to-end, and see the model in action. This is your chance to validate everything you've read.

05

Award & Site Selection

Sign the franchise agreement, receive your territory, and begin disciplined site selection with our real estate team. We help you find a location that meets WaveMAX standards.

06

Build, Train & Launch

Build-out coordination, equipment installation, staff hiring and training, marketing launch playbook, and grand opening — followed by ongoing operational support as you ramp.

The numbers

What does it cost to buy a WaveMAX®?

Total investment for a WaveMAX laundromat franchise typically ranges from $1 million to $1.5 million, depending on your market, real estate costs, build-out scope, and equipment configuration. This range covers everything required to open the doors.

What's included

Franchise fees, lease and build-out, all commercial Electrolux equipment, signage and branding, technology and POS, opening inventory, and pre-opening marketing.

What's required

Minimum $400,000 in liquid cash for investment and $1 million minimum net worth. Most franchisees finance a portion of the equipment and use SBA-eligible financing for the build-out.

Lender network

We work with a network of laundromat-friendly lenders who understand the unit economics of the WaveMAX model and can structure financing accordingly.

Full pro forma

During discovery, we walk you through sources and uses of capital, projected ramp curve, debt service coverage, and a clear picture of your monthly cash flow in years one and two.

Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

How much does it cost to buy a WaveMAX laundromat franchise?

Total investment ranges from $1 million to $1.5 million depending on location, build-out, and equipment. Minimum liquidity is $400,000 with a $1 million net worth requirement. Most franchisees use SBA financing for a portion of the build-out and equipment.

How long does the application process take?

Our franchise discovery process typically takes 60–120 days from initial conversation to franchise award, depending on how quickly you complete each phase. We move at the pace of the qualified candidate, not the sales calendar.

Can I open multiple WaveMAX locations?

Yes. WaveMAX is built for multi-unit ownership with structured area development agreements and growth incentives for qualified operators. Many of our most successful franchisees scale from one to multiple locations.

Do I need laundromat experience?

No prior laundromat experience is required. Our training program, operations manual, and ongoing coaching are designed to take qualified business owners from zero to operational confidence. What we look for is operating discipline and coachability.

What ongoing fees does WaveMAX charge?

WaveMAX charges a royalty fee on gross sales and a marketing fund contribution. Specific fee percentages, payment terms, and any ancillary fees are detailed in our Franchise Disclosure Document, which we share early in the discovery process.

What happens if it isn't the right fit?

You walk away. We expect that of qualified candidates and we expect that of ourselves. Better to find out in week 2 than in year 2. The owners who make it all the way through the discovery process are the ones who genuinely belong in the system.
