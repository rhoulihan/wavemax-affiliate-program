Our story

Built to modernize an industry stuck in the past.

WaveMAX was founded in 2012 by Mike Roberts in Jacksonville, Florida. After two decades building successful businesses across multiple industries, Mike became fascinated with the laundromat industry's stability and cash flow potential — and equally struck by how outdated the average laundromat had become as a customer experience.

The thesis behind WaveMAX: laundry is a steady, recession-resistant business, but most laundromats run as broken legacy operations — neglected equipment, poor lighting, no staffing, no systems. Modernize the design, professionalize the operations, and franchise the playbook, and the result is a category-defining experience that customers actually choose, not just settle for.

Today the system operates as AU Hydro LLC dba WaveMAX Laundry, with 75+ open locations and a growing network of independent franchise owners across the United States.

I founded WaveMAX with a simple mission: to help entrepreneurs build profitable, sustainable laundromat businesses that deliver superior returns while enhancing their lifestyle rather than consuming it.

Mike Roberts Founder & CEO, WaveMAX Laundry

What we stand for

A national network. One operating standard.

Every WaveMAX is independently owned and operated, but the system runs on a shared set of operating standards. These values shape both the customer experience inside the store and the long-term partnership we build with each owner.

Cleaner.

Modern equipment, attended floors, advanced sanitation in equipped stores. The customer should never have to wonder whether the machine is clean enough to use.

Safer.

Bright stores, attended hours, and disciplined site selection that puts WaveMAX locations in neighborhoods that read as safe to families running errands at night.

Faster.

High-extraction commercial machines move loads through faster than home machines or typical laundromats. Less waiting, more done.

Brand integrity.

We are intentionally selective about who we award territories to. Every owner reflects the WaveMAX standard in their market.

Owner outcomes first.

The success metric for any decision is whether it builds franchisee cash flow over the long term — not short-term royalty extraction.

Operating discipline.

Site selection, equipment, training, marketing, and operations all run on documented playbooks. Repeatable across markets, not bespoke.
