WHY WAVEMAX

Why we chose WaveMAX

We're three Austin locals who wanted to invest in our neighborhood — not just write a check, but build something that delivers real value to the people who live here.

WaveMAX is the platform that lets us do that: best-in-class equipment, hospital-grade water sanitation, and a service model designed to keep prices low and quality high. We get to put our values into practice — community first, quality always — and people get the cleanest, fastest, friendliest laundry experience in Central Austin.

Proud to be serving the Central Austin community since 2025.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our values, on every wash

  • Community first. Profits reinvested locally — staff, equipment, and supporting Austin causes that matter to us.
  • Best-in-class quality. Omni LUX UV-sanitized water, eco-friendly hypoallergenic detergent, calibrated weighs, hand-folded results.
  • Lowest fair price. No hidden fees, no contracts, no subscriptions. Cards-only, transparent pricing — pay for what you use.
  • Always open. 7am–10pm, every day, 365 days a year. We're here when laundry day actually happens.
  • Friendly service. Fully attended store. Real people, not a vending machine. Need help? We're here.
