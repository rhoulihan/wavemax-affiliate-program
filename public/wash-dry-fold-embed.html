How fast is the turnaround?

Most orders are ready in 24 hours. Drop off before 11am and we can usually have it ready the same evening. We text you when it's ready.

Is there a minimum order size?

Yes — 10 lb minimum (~$12 at the $1.20/lb rate). For reference, one full grocery bag of laundry typically weighs 8–12 lb.

What about delicates and dry-clean only items?

Flag them at drop-off and we'll air-dry, hand-fold, and put them on hangers. We don't currently dry-clean — those items should go to a dedicated dry cleaner.

How are loads separated?

Whites and colors are washed in separate machines. Your order is never mixed with another customer's. Each bag is tagged at intake.

What if something goes missing?

We log the weight + bag count at intake, and folded items are bagged together at completion. If you notice something missing, call us within 7 days and we'll trace it.

When can I drop off?

We're open 7am–10pm, every day, 365 days a year. Drop-off is fully attended — we don't have an after-hours slot, but our hours cover most schedules.
