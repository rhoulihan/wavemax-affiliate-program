WAVEMAX LAUNDRY PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: May 31, 2025
Last Updated: May 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how WaveMAX Laundry ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you participate in our affiliate program or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register as an affiliate or customer, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Account credentials (username, password)
  • Payment information (bank account details, tax ID)
  • Business information (company name, type of business)
  • Profile information and preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage patterns and navigation data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Log files and analytics data

1.3 Social Media Information

If you choose to register or login using social media platforms (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn), we may collect:

  • Basic profile information (name, email address)
  • Profile picture (if publicly available)
  • Social media account identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your affiliate or customer account
  • Service Delivery: Processing orders, scheduling pickups, and providing laundry services
  • Commission Payments: Calculating and processing affiliate commissions
  • Communication: Sending notifications, updates, and promotional materials
  • Customer Support: Responding to inquiries and providing assistance
  • Analytics: Improving our services and understanding usage patterns
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and preventing fraud
  • Marketing: Sending relevant offers and promotional content (with consent)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing and financial services
  • Email delivery and communication services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support and chat services
  • Cloud hosting and data storage

3.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

3.4 Affiliate Program

Limited customer information may be shared with affiliates for order management and customer service purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access and authentication requirements
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and audit logging
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling security breaches

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze website usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content and experiences
  • Enable social media features
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about how we process your data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Typically, we retain account information for 7 years after account closure, and transaction records for 10 years as required by law.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by regulatory authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Binding corporate rules
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected and used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

11. European Privacy Rights

European Union residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including those outlined in Section 6 above. Our lawful basis for processing includes:

  • Consent for marketing communications
  • Contract performance for service delivery
  • Legitimate interests for analytics and fraud prevention
  • Legal obligations for compliance requirements

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices in our applications

Your continued use of our services after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Third-Party Links

Our services may contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.
