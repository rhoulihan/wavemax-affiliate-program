How the partnership works

Start to finish — from the bags in your hand to the check in your mailbox.

  1. 01

    Get your bags + QR codes

    We hand you durable laundry bags, each printed with its own QR code. No machines to buy, no plant to build.

  2. 02

    Set your pickup & delivery rules

    Tell us your pickup windows, delivery notes, and the area you serve. Your customers, your terms.

  3. 03

    Customers register a bag

    Your customer scans the QR on their bag and signs up in under a minute. The bag is theirs — the relationship is yours.

  4. 04

    Run pickup & delivery

    Collect full bags from your customers and bring them to our plant. You own the route and every customer on it.

  5. 05

    Set your own fees

    You decide what to charge. The margin between your price and our wholesale wash rate is yours to keep.

  6. 06

    We pay you by check

    We process your customers' payments and mail you a check. No invoicing, no chasing money, no middle-man taking a cut.
