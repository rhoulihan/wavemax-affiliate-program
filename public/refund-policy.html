WAVEMAX LAUNDRY REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: January 2, 2025
Last Updated: January 2, 2025

At WaveMAX Laundry, we are committed to providing exceptional wash, dry, and fold laundry services. This policy outlines our refund practices and our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Non-Refundable Services

Due to the nature of our wash, dry, and fold laundry services, all services rendered are non-refundable. This policy applies to:

  • Completed wash, dry, and fold services
  • Pickup and delivery services
  • Any specialty treatments or services
  • Prepaid service packages or subscriptions
  • Affiliate registration fees (if applicable)

Important: Once your laundry has been processed, cleaned, dried, and folded, we cannot offer refunds as the service has been completed.

Our Commitment to Customer Satisfaction

While our services are non-refundable, we are always willing to work proactively with our customers to ensure complete satisfaction. If you experience any issues with our service, we are here to help resolve them.

When you contact us with a concern, our team will work with you to:

  • Offer complimentary re-cleaning or re-processing if service quality was below our standards
  • Provide service credits for future orders when warranted
  • Address any damage or loss claims according to our damage policy

We maintain high standards for all our services:

  • Timely Service: We strive to meet all pickup and delivery time commitments

Cancellation Policy

Before Service Begins

You may cancel your scheduled pickup or service before your items are collected:

  • No fees apply for cancellations made at least 2 hours before scheduled pickup
  • Contact us immediately if you need to cancel a scheduled service
  • Prepaid amounts will be credited to your account for future use

After Service Begins

Once your laundry has been picked up or processing has begun:

  • Cancellations are not available as the service is already in progress
  • You will be responsible for payment of services rendered
  • If you have concerns during processing, please contact support immediately

Damage and Loss Claims

In the unlikely event of damage or loss to your items:

  • Report the issue to support@wavemax.promo within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos and description of the damaged or missing items
  • Include original purchase receipts if available
  • Our team will investigate and respond with a resolution

Claims are evaluated on a case-by-case basis, and we will work to reach a fair resolution.

Payment Issues

If you believe you were incorrectly charged or have billing questions:

  • Contact our support team at support@wavemax.promo
  • Provide your order number and details of the billing concern
  • We will review your account and issue corrections or credits as appropriate
  • Billing disputes should be reported within 30 days of the charge
