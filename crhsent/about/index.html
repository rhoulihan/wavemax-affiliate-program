FIG.03 / The thesis

One model, not five.

Most data platforms bolt together five specialized stores — relational, document, graph, time-series, search — and a web of pipelines to keep them in sync. Unified Model Theory holds that those are projections of one canonical form, not separate databases: one source of truth, zero sync, and — critically for AI — no eventual-consistency gap where a model reads stale data and hallucinates.
